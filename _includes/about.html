Developer Bio

I started programming with my best friend in primary school. Back in 1991, we were coding music and simple games in BASIC for the Commodore 64. Later, while studying Political Science and Philosophy, I began freelancing in web development. I have been crafting websites since the early 2000s, and I still remember the shock when CSS was introduced.

Today, I specialize in JavaScript technologies (React, Node.js, Three.js, TypeScript, Vue.js, Angular, etc.), having been part of the tech industry since 2014. Over the past decade, I have worked both as a freelancer and within respected companies.

I am also the founder of School of Code in Belgrade, where we offer specialized training for individuals and companies.
